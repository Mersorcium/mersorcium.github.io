Mersorcium is an international network of researchers specialized or interested in research related to mercury cycling in the environment. We offer a platform for researchers interested in mercury research to share information, communicate and harness potential collaborations.

Join our mailing list to become a member of Mersorcium and receive newsletters. You can also message the group through the google group email address to share information about conferences, publications and job positions and suggest collaborative projects! Finally, enjoy our literature survey about Hg cycling in aquatic and terrestrial ecosystems in the dedicated google scholar survey here.
